About me

I’m currently a PhD student Mathematics and Modeling in Montpellier, France.

My thesis is about "Numerical simulation of intracardiac hemodynamics". I am under the supervision of Franck Nicoud at Institut Montpelliérain Alexander Grothendieck (IMAG - University of Montpellier).

Research interests
